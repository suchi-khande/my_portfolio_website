Diabeties prediction

In my diabetes prediction project, I focus on developing a machine learning model to predict the likelihood of diabetes based on patient data. This involves collecting and preprocessing data, such as blood glucose levels, BMI, age, and family history, to create a clean dataset for analysis. I use algorithms like logistic regression, decision trees, or neural networks to build predictive models. Model performance is evaluated using metrics like accuracy, precision, recall, and the ROC curve. The goal is to create a reliable tool that can assist healthcare professionals in early diagnosis and personalized treatment plans for diabetes patients.
